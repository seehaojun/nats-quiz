# P4 Quiz App -- MOE Syllabus Gap Analysis

**Date:** 2026-03-27
**Scope:** Compare existing quiz content against the Singapore MOE Primary 4 syllabus for Science, English, Math, and Chinese.

---

## 1. SCIENCE

### Currently Covered (5 themes, 17 data files)

| Theme | File | Categories | Topics |
|-------|------|-----------|--------|
| Diversity | `diversity.js` | ch1 (Classifying Things), ch2 (Living & Non-Living), ch3 (Plants), tricky, notqs, plantsys, apply, hard, compare | Classification, living vs non-living characteristics, groups of living things (plants, animals, fungi, bacteria), plant parts & uses, extinct animals |
| Cycles | `cycles.js` | ch1, ch2, ch3, tricky, hard, compare | Water cycle (evaporation, condensation, precipitation, collection), life cycles of butterflies/beetles/cockroaches/grasshoppers/frogs, complete vs incomplete metamorphosis, plant life cycles |
| Systems | `systems.js` | ch1, ch2, ch3, ch4, tricky, hard, compare | Digestive system, respiratory system, circulatory system, plant transport system (roots, stem, xylem), electrical circuits (open/closed, switches, batteries) |
| Energy | `energy.js` | ch1, ch2, ch3, ch4, tricky, hard, compare | Light (luminous/non-luminous, transparent/translucent/opaque, shadows, reflection), heat (conduction, insulation, conductors/insulators, temperature, changes of state), photosynthesis, forces (push/pull, friction) |
| Interactions | `interactions.js` | ch1, ch2, ch3, tricky, hard, compare | Food chains & food webs, producers/consumers/decomposers, herbivores/carnivores/omnivores, predator-prey, adaptations (structural & behavioural), camouflage, hibernation, migration, environmental conservation (pollution, 3Rs, deforestation, endangered species) |

### GAPS in Science

| Gap | Priority | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| **Matter (States of Matter)** | HIGH | The 2024 P4 syllabus explicitly covers matter -- properties of solids, liquids, and gases; changes of state (melting, freezing, boiling, evaporation, condensation). While some changes of state appear under Energy/Heat, there is no dedicated Matter section covering the three states, their properties (definite/indefinite shape and volume), and classifying materials. |
| **Magnets** | HIGH | Magnets are a core P3-P4 topic: properties of magnets, magnetic vs non-magnetic materials, poles, attraction/repulsion, uses of magnets. Completely absent from the app. |
| **Process Skills / Scientific Investigation** | MEDIUM | P4 exams commonly test process skills: fair testing (variables to keep the same/change/measure), making predictions, drawing conclusions from data/tables/graphs. No questions on experimental design. |
| **Forces -- Gravity** | MEDIUM | Only friction is covered. Gravity as a force pulling objects toward Earth, weight vs mass, and effects of gravity are missing. |
| **Forces -- Elastic Spring Force** | LOW | Springs and elastic materials (stretching, compressing) are sometimes tested at P4 level. |

### ENRICHMENT for Science

- **Diagram-based questions:** P4 Science exams heavily use diagrams (circuit diagrams, food web diagrams, water cycle diagrams, plant diagrams). The MCQ format cannot show images, but text-described diagram questions could simulate this.
- **"Why" and "Explain" questions:** The app focuses on factual recall. Adding more application questions that ask students to explain phenomena (e.g., "Why does the ice cream melt faster on a metal plate than a wooden plate?") would better prepare for exams.
- **Open-ended format questions:** P4 SA2 papers include open-ended sections worth significant marks. While the app is MCQ-only, questions can be structured to test reasoning (which the tricky/hard categories already do well).

## 2. ENGLISH

### Currently Covered (4 themes)

| Theme | File | Categories | Topics |
|-------|------|-----------|--------|
| Grammar | `grammar.js` | ch1 (Tenses, 25q), ch2 (Parts of Speech, 25q), ch3 (Sentence Structure, 20q), tricky (15q), hard (15q), compare (15q) | Simple present/past/future, continuous tenses, present perfect, past perfect, subject-verb agreement, question tags, reported speech, passive voice, conditionals |
| Vocabulary | `vocabulary.js` | ch1 (Word Meanings, 25q), ch2 (Synonyms & Antonyms, 20q), ch3 (Phrasal Verbs & Idioms, 20q), tricky (15q), hard (15q), compare (10q) | Contextual word meanings, synonyms, antonyms, phrasal verbs, idioms, commonly confused words |
| Comprehension | `comprehension.js` | ch1 (Main Idea & Details, 20q), ch2 (Inference, 20q), tricky (10q), hard (10q), compare (10q) | Literal comprehension, inferential comprehension, comparison passages |
| Synthesis & Transformation | `synthesis.js` | ch1 (Sentence Combining, 20q), ch2 (Transformation, 20q), tricky (10q), hard (10q) | Conjunctions (although, because, so, or), correlative conjunctions (both...and, neither...nor, not only...but also), passive voice, reported speech, too...to, so...that, enough...to, inversion |

### GAPS in English

| Gap | Priority | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| **Editing / Proofreading** | HIGH | A standard P4 Paper 2 component (4 marks). Students must identify and correct spelling and grammatical errors in a passage. Completely absent from the app. |
| **Visual Text Comprehension** | HIGH | A standard P4 Paper 2 component (8 marks, 8 questions). Students read posters, advertisements, flyers, notices, and answer questions about them. Completely absent. |
| **Vocabulary Cloze (open-ended)** | HIGH | A standard P4 Paper 2 component (5 marks). Students fill in blanks in a passage using their own vocabulary (no options given). Different from grammar cloze. The app has no open-ended cloze. |
| **Grammar Cloze (passage-based)** | MEDIUM | P4 Paper 2 includes grammar cloze passages (8 marks) where students select the correct word to fill blanks in a passage. The app tests grammar in isolated sentences, not in passage context. |
| **Composition / Writing** | LOW | P4 Paper 1 includes picture composition and situational writing. While hard to test in MCQ format, questions about writing techniques (narrative structure, descriptive language, essay organisation) could be added. |
| **Listening Comprehension** | LOW | Part of P4 assessment but not feasible in a text-based quiz app. |
| **Oral / Speaking** | LOW | Not feasible in text format. |
| **Punctuation** | MEDIUM | Basic punctuation rules (commas, full stops, question marks, speech marks) are tested in editing. No dedicated punctuation questions exist. |
| **Articles (a, an, the)** | MEDIUM | While some grammar questions touch on this, there is no dedicated section for article usage, which is commonly tested. |

### ENRICHMENT for English

- **Cloze passage format:** Instead of isolated sentences, present a short paragraph with multiple blanks. This mirrors the actual exam format.
- **Error identification MCQs:** "Which underlined word contains an error?" format to simulate editing.
- **Visual text simulation:** Describe a poster/advertisement in text and ask questions about it (date, venue, price, target audience, purpose).
- **Word order / sentence rearrangement:** Arrange jumbled words into a correct sentence.

## 3. MATHEMATICS

### Currently Covered (4 themes)

| Theme | File | Categories | Topics |
|-------|------|-----------|--------|
| Whole Numbers | `wholenumbers.js` | ch1 (Place Value & Ordering, 25q), ch2 (Operations & Word Problems, 25q), ch3 (Factors & Multiples, 20q), tricky, hard, compare | Place value up to 100,000; rounding; ordering; patterns; multiplication & division up to 4 digits; estimation; factors, multiples, primes, composites |
| Fractions | `fractions.js` | ch1 (Equivalent Fractions & Basics, 20q), ch2 (Addition & Subtraction, 25q), ch3 (Decimals, 20q), tricky, hard, compare | Equivalent fractions, mixed numbers, improper fractions, comparing & ordering, fraction of a set, adding/subtracting unlike fractions, decimals to 2 places, decimal addition/subtraction |
| Geometry | `geometry.js` | ch1 (Angles, 20q), ch2 (Symmetry & Tessellation, 20q), ch3 (Area & Perimeter, 25q), tricky, hard, compare | Types of angles, angles on straight line, angles at a point, lines of symmetry, tessellation, area & perimeter of rectangles/squares, composite figures |
| Tables & Graphs | `tables.js` | ch1 (Tables, 15q), ch2 (Bar & Line Graphs, 20q), tricky, hard, compare | Reading tables, two-way tables, bar graphs, line graphs, interpreting data, averages |

### GAPS in Math

| Gap | Priority | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| **Decimals (dedicated section)** | HIGH | Decimals are a major P4 topic but currently embedded as a small ch3 inside Fractions. Missing: decimal place value (tenths, hundredths, thousandths), decimal notation, comparing/ordering decimals, converting between fractions and decimals, multiplying/dividing decimals, rounding decimals. |
| **Measurement: Length, Mass, Volume** | HIGH | Completely absent. P4 covers metric conversion (km/m/cm/mm, kg/g, L/mL), word problems involving measurement, and converting between units. |
| **Money** | HIGH | Not covered. P4 tests money calculations, making change, word problems involving dollars and cents, conversion between dollars and cents. |
| **Time** | MEDIUM | Moved to P3 under 2024 syllabus but still tested in P4 exams as prior knowledge. 12-hour and 24-hour clock, duration, elapsed time, and time word problems are absent. |
| **Pie Charts** | MEDIUM | New to P4 under the 2024 syllabus revision (moved from P6). Reading and interpreting pie charts with numbers or fractions. Completely absent. |
| **Nets of Solids** | MEDIUM | New to P4 under the 2024 syllabus (moved from P6). Identifying nets of prisms and pyramids. Absent. |
| **Fraction Multiplication** | MEDIUM | Multiplying a fraction by a whole number (e.g., 3 x 2/5) is a P4 topic. Some questions touch on "fraction of a set" but not explicit fraction multiplication. |
| **Word Problems (Model Method)** | MEDIUM | The Singapore Math model/bar method for solving word problems is a core P4 skill. While some word problems exist, there are no questions specifically teaching or testing model-drawing logic. |
| **Patterns and Sequences** | LOW | Number patterns (completing sequences, finding rules) have minimal coverage. |

### ENRICHMENT for Math

- **Multi-step word problems:** P4 exams feature multi-step problems worth 3-5 marks. More complex word problems combining operations would help.
- **"Explain your reasoning" questions:** E.g., "Ali says 1/3 is greater than 1/2 because 3 is greater than 2. Is he correct? Explain."
- **Heuristics questions:** Guess-and-check, work backwards, draw a model, look for patterns -- these problem-solving strategies are core to P4 Math.

## 4. CHINESE

### Currently Covered (4 themes)

| Theme | File | Categories | Topics |
|-------|------|-----------|--------|
| 汉字 Words | `hanzi.js` | ch1 (Word Recognition, 25q), ch2 (Synonyms & Antonyms, 20q), ch3 (Measure Words & Collocations, 20q), tricky, hard | Contextual word choice, character disambiguation, word meanings, 近义词/反义词, 量词 |
| 语法 Grammar | `yufa.js` | ch1 (Sentence Structure, 20q), ch2 (Connectors, 20q), ch3 (Punctuation, 15q), tricky, hard | 把字句, 被字句, word order, 因为...所以, 虽然...但是, 不但...而且, 如果...就, 不管...还是, punctuation marks |
| 完形填空 Cloze | `cloze.js` | ch1 (Word Choice, 25q), ch2 (Context Understanding, 20q), tricky, hard | Fill-in-the-blank word selection, contextual vocabulary, adverbs, adjectives in context |
| 阅读理解 Comprehension | `yuedu.js` | ch1 (Short Passage, 20q), ch2 (Inference, 15q), tricky, hard | Literal comprehension (factual recall), inferential comprehension, logical reasoning from passages |

### GAPS in Chinese

| Gap | Priority | Notes |
|-----|----------|-------|
| **作文 (Composition / Writing)** | HIGH | Paper 1 of the Chinese exam (worth 40 marks, 34% of total). Includes 看图作文 (picture composition) and 命题作文 (topic composition). While MCQ cannot fully test writing, questions on narrative sequencing, descriptive vocabulary selection, and 好词好句 (good phrases) could help. |
| **听力 (Listening Comprehension)** | HIGH | Paper 3 (worth 26 marks, 25% of total). Not feasible in current text-based format but represents a major exam component. |
| **口试 (Oral Communication)** | HIGH | Paper 4 (worth 40 marks). Includes 朗读 (reading aloud), 看图说话 (picture discussion), and 会话 (conversation). Not feasible in text format. |
| **成语 (Chinese Idioms / Chengyu)** | MEDIUM | Commonly tested in P4 Chinese exams. The app has no dedicated section for 四字成语 and their usage in context. |
| **句式转换 (Sentence Transformation)** | MEDIUM | Converting between sentence types (e.g., 把 to 被, statement to question, positive to negative). Some exists under Grammar but could be expanded. |
| **多音字 (Characters with Multiple Pronunciations)** | LOW | Characters that change pronunciation based on context (e.g., 长 chang/zhang, 好 hao3/hao4). Only a few questions touch on this. |
| **偏旁部首 (Radicals)** | LOW | Understanding Chinese character radicals helps with character recognition and dictionary skills. The app's description mentions "radicals, stroke order" but actual content is focused on word choice. |
| **词语搭配 (Collocations beyond Measure Words)** | LOW | Verb-object collocations (e.g., 做功课, 打电话, 放假) and adjective-noun collocations are commonly tested. |

### ENRICHMENT for Chinese

- **成语故事 (Idiom stories):** Present a short story and ask which 成语 best describes the situation.
- **改错题 (Error correction):** Similar to English editing -- find and correct wrong characters or grammar in a passage.
- **排列句子 (Sentence ordering):** Arrange jumbled sentences into a logical paragraph.
- **看图选词 (Picture-based vocabulary):** Describe a scene and choose the most appropriate word/phrase.

## 7. RECOMMENDED NEXT STEPS

1. **Add new Math themes:** `decimals` (dedicated), `measurement`, `money` -- these fill the largest content gap.
2. **Add new English themes:** `editing` (proofreading), `visualtext` (visual text comprehension) -- these are standard exam components with zero coverage.
3. **Add new Science themes or expand Energy:** Add a `matter` theme (states of matter) and a `magnets` theme.
4. **Expand existing themes:** Add pie chart questions to Tables & Graphs, add nets questions to Geometry, add idiom questions to Chinese.
5. **Consider exam-format questions:** Where possible, structure questions to mimic actual P4 exam formats (passage-based cloze, error identification, multi-step word problems).
